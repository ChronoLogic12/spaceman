Spaceman - How to play

Crack the code and guess the space themed word to gain access to the rocket before it leaves on its adventure without you. The word you need to guess is represented by a series of dashes, each representing a letter. Attempt to guess the word by selecting one letter at a time from the on screen keyboard. Correct guesses will reveal the letter or letters within the word while incorrect guesses will bring the countdown to lift off closer to 0, whether you are ready or not.
